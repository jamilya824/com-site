Requirements for admission
a) Requirements on the specialty:

Bachelor's degree or computer scientist with 75% performance over the past two years. A bachelor's degree or a technical degree is also accepted with an academic achievement of 85% in subjects related to computer science over the last two years of training. These competences must be documented. Professional programming activities can be recognized as fulfilling the criterion for admission. If necessary, the student must listen to the courses on in-depth programming and software development in the Bachelor's degree program of KGPPI or Alatoo University. These compulsory courses are read not in the evening or on the weekends. For possible courses, a fee is charged in addition to the principal amount of the tuition fee.

b) Requirements for foreign languages:

▪ English B2
▪ English B1 or German B2
The level of English is determined by the teachers of the University of Alatoo.
The level of German language proficiency is determined by the teachers of KGFPI KGUSTA

List of documents required for admission

1. Application
2. The original of the bachelor's degree (specialist) with the application of the extract from the record sheet
3. Copy of the Diploma Supplement
4. 2 pictures of 3x4
5. Copy of passport (original for viewing)
6. The contract
7. Personal account of personnel
